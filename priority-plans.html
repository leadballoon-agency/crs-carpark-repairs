Common Questions

How do Priority Plans work? +
Priority Plans provide scheduled maintenance and priority response for a fixed monthly fee. You'll receive regular inspections, discounted repairs, and guaranteed response times.
Can I change plans later? +
Yes! You can upgrade at any time, or downgrade with 30 days notice. We'll work with you to find the right level of service.
What's included in minor repairs? +
Minor repairs include crack filling, small pothole patches, and preventative maintenance. Major resurfacing or structural work would be quoted separately with your plan discount applied.
Is there a contract commitment? +
Plans operate on a rolling monthly basis with no long-term commitment required. Simply provide 30 days notice if you wish to cancel.
