Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our service

Why zone-based pricing instead of per-pothole? +

Zone-based pricing is more efficient and cost-effective. Setting up equipment, materials, and crew costs the same whether we fix 1 or 10 potholes. Plus, damage spreads - fixing one pothole while ignoring nearby damage means we'll be back next year. Our zone approach fixes everything once, properly, with a 5-year warranty.

How accurate is the AI detection? +

Our AI is trained on thousands of UK car park images and achieves 94% accuracy in detecting potholes, cracks, and surface damage. It actually finds damage humans often miss. The AI gives you an instant estimate, then our team validates during the site assessment to ensure 100% accuracy on the final quote.

What's included in the 5-year warranty? +

Our warranty covers all repaired areas against cracking, sinking, and deterioration for 5 full years. If any repair fails, we fix it free of charge. This includes labor and materials. The only exclusions are damage from accidents, extreme weather events (flooding), or underground utility work.

How quickly can you start? +

For standard jobs (under 500m¬≤), we can usually start within 5 business days. Emergency repairs can be done within 24-48 hours. We work weekends and evenings to minimize disruption to your business. Most jobs are completed in 1-2 days.

Do you work with insurance claims? +

Yes, we work with all major insurers. Our detailed photo documentation and zone-based quotes are exactly what insurers need. We can bill insurance directly or provide all documentation for your claim. The AI tool's analysis report is accepted by most insurers as initial evidence.
